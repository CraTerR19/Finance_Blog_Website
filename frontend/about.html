The Person Behind the Screen

Every week, I analyze the most significant news and data to bring you actionable insights with the help of AI and other resources (I'm not a financial advisor, just a finance enthusiast). Whether it's shifting monetary policies, earnings reports, or unprecedented market swings, my goal is to help you understand the "why" behind the numbers.
